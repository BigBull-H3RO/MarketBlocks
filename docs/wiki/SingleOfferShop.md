# SingleOfferShop

The **SingleOfferShop** is a block-based shop with exactly one active offer per shop. It comes in different block variants, including the classic **Trade Stand** and the **Market Crate**.

## Quick Overview

- **One offer** with up to 2 payment stacks and 1 result stack
- **Block variants**: Trade Stand (two blocks tall with optional showcase) and Market Crate (single block)
- **Clear owner/role model** with primary owner and up to 10 co-owners
- **Access control**: Restrict buyers via whitelist, blacklist, or open to everyone
- **Shop name**: Name your shop (max 32 characters); close/open your shop at will
- **Shift-click bulk purchases**: Buy multiple units at once with automatic limit calculation
- **6 configurable settings tabs**: General, I/O, Villager/NPC, Visuals, Notifications, and Access
- **Visual NPCs**: Display interactive Villagers or Player skins above the shop
- **Offer item rendering**: Show the offered item floating above the shop with full customization
- **Offline notifications**: Get alerted when you log in if your shop is out of stock or output is full
- **I/O system**: Configure side-based hopper/chest input/output with redstone control
- **Transaction log**: Every purchase is logged with buyer, items, timestamp, and smart stacking
- **Redstone integration**: Emit a redstone pulse on purchase; read fill level via comparator
- **Admin shop mode**: Server-controlled shops with unlimited stock
- **Advancements**: Unlock achievements as you explore shop features
- **Server-side validation**: All critical actions are validated on the server
- **Configurable blast resistance**: Protect shops from explosions (default: bedrock-level)
